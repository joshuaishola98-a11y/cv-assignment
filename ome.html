Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omellete combimes beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optically filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or

Preparation time

  1. Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  2. Preparation: 5 minutes
  3. Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, vegetables, herbs, ham, etc.

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk (if using), salt, and pepper until well combined.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add the butter or oil.
  3. Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the egg mixture.
  4. Let the eggs cook undisturbed for about 1-2 minutes until the edges start to set.
  5. If using fillings, sprinkle them over one half of the omelette.
  6. Using a spatula, gently fold the omelette in half over the fillings.
  7. Cook for another minute until the inside is cooked to your liking.
  8. Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve hot.
